Ladylike

Click in the above box to play

Originally made for Ludum Dare 27, Aug 24-26 2013 - Emmett Butler, Nina Freeman, audio by Deckman Coss

Updated version made for IGF Student Showcase - Emmett Butler, Nina Freeman, audio by Deckman Coss, and visuals by Winnie Song. Ladylike received an honorable mention in the 2014 IGF Student Showcase.

This game was made using Flixel, Photoshop, the GNU Image Manipulation Program, Twine, and Twine for Actionscript. You can view the Github repository.
